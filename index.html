Why I made it

Most mood apps ask you to keep streaks and come back every day or feel like you have failed. That never helped me. It just became one more thing to keep up with.

So I built Inner Forecast around a gentler idea: feelings move like weather. You do not need to fix the sky to notice it. You just look up, name it kindly, and carry on.

No subscription

No subscription, no ads, no account, no trackers, and no data selling. There is nothing to sign up for and no server collecting anything.

Your wellbeing should not sit behind a monthly fee. Inner Forecast is £1.99 and then it's yours, forever.

Private by design

Everything you log stays on your device, encrypted, and kept out of iCloud and backups. The app makes no network requests for its core features and uses no third party code.

Apple Health is optional and off by default. When on, the app only adds the check-ins you create and never reads your Health data.
