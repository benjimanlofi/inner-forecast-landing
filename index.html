Why I made it

Most mood apps ask for streaks and daily check-ins, and make you feel like you have failed when life gets in the way. That never helped me. It became one more thing to keep up with.

So I built the gentler app I wanted: miss a week and nothing breaks, log twice an hour and nothing judges. It is just you, noticing.
